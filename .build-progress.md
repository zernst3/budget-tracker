  **OPEN — convention precedence note for Zach (ROUTE-1):**
  `RUST-SEAORM-PROJECTION-TYPES-1` literally says a projection "lives in the
  domain crate AND derives the `SeaORM` result-mapping trait." That conflicts
  with `RUST-DOMAIN-1` (domain crate forbids any `SeaORM` dep; `Cargo.toml`
  enforces it; the crate must stay WASM-clean). I reconciled by putting the
  projection STRUCTS in `budget-domain` (pure types, satisfying "lives in the
  domain crate" + `RUST-SEAORM-RAW-SQL-ESCAPE-1`'s "only typed domain types
  cross the trait boundary") while the `FromQueryResult` derive sits on a private
  infra-local row struct that maps into them. `RUST-DOMAIN-1` (structural,
  enforced) wins over the literal "derives the trait here" clause. This needs
  Zach's ratification (a one-line amendment to the projection rule) since it sets
  the precedent for every future projection.
